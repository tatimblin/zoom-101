Download the zoom video calling app

Before your video call:

  1. Download the zoom app

    • iPhone / iPad
    • Android
    • Computer

To start your call

  1. Check your email or texts for a message similar to this, and click the link

    After clicking the link, the zoom app will automatically open on your device

  2. Zoom needs access to your camera to video chat, click ok

  3. Click the blue join with video button, to start video chat

  4. Don't allow Zoom to send notifications

  5. Zoom needs access to your microphone for you to be able to talk, click ok

  6. Next, click 'join audio' at the bottom of the screen

  7. Lastly, confirm by clicking 'Call using internet audio'. You should now be video chatting with your doctor
